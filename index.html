The 4 Phase Process

Emails, Just Emails.

We are precise and thorough in doing ONLY this one thing – and with such monastic focus to the point of being better than anyone else. If you want an agency that offers a full service solution of everything that won’t move the needle forward, we’re not for you.

If you want an agency that clearly contributes to increasing your store’s monthly profits – we’re for you.

PHASE 1 (RESEARCH)
PHASE 2 (PLANNING)
PHASE 3 (IMPLEMENTATION)
PHASE 4 (SCALING)

PHASE 1 (RESEARCH)

First, we get to know your business inside and out to ensure we have a clear understanding of what you sell, who your target marketing is and your goals for your business.

After you are onboarding call, we start market research on your niche, products, customers and competitors to tailor an email marketing strategy made JUST FOR YOU.

We also do this to ensure that we are planning and deploying emails that your target audience will enjoy/resonate with.

PHASE 2 (PLANNING)

In this phase, we create your custom email plan based on the market research and information we get from you. We plan every email inside every flow in detail so our copywriters and designers are ready to implement.

For every client, we create a campaign calendar in advance and follow that for all of the well-designed campaigns we would be sending out.

PHASE 3 (IMPLEMENTATION)

In this phase, we are implementing the emails that we mapped out in the early phase. We start building out automation on Klaviyo, setting up the core emails your store needs and the email campaigns ready.

Our in house account managers, copywriters, designers, coders are all working together to implement all emails that are inside of the flows

In the implementation phase, there is a lot of work involved as we have to create branded content and email copy for each email that belongs in a Klaviyo sequence. This is where we do early split tests, A/B subject lines tests, segment current data and email user, etc.

PHASE 4 (SCALING)

During phase 4, our goal is to make conclusions based on data. Every email flow will be implemented, campaigns would be going out regularly and we would find new ways to improve the quality of emails and strategies we have implemented

We gather email data after a period of time and  analyze that data to update email sequences, apply new segmentations and update our email campaign approach

During this phase, we are actively cleaning up the list to make sure it is up to date and as effective as possible.
